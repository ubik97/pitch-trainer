Pitch Practice

An online tool for achieving perfect pitch!

Sharps/Flats?















Hello!

This is a little web game designed to help you develop perfect pitch. To get started, first select whether you want the game to quiz to include sharps and flats. (Hint: If you're a beginner, start out with just the naturals, it's easier). Then select start. Listen to the tone and play the note you think it is. The game will tell you if you are correct. To play another note, press the next button and repeat. When you are done, press finished to get a summary of your progress.

Thanks for playing, and have fun!
