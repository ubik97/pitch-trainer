Pitch Practice

An online tool for achieving perfect pitch!

Sharps/Flats?















Hello!

This is a little web game designed to help you develop perfect pitch. To get started, first select a game mode. You can play with only naturals, only sharps/flats, or both! (Hint: If you're a beginner, start out with naturals or sharps/flats, both is harder). Then select start. Listen to the tone and play the note you think it is. The game will tell you if you are correct. To play another note, press the next button and repeat. When you are done, press finish to get a summary of your progress. Also, it is reccomended that your keep your volume on 10 or below, as the keyboard can be pretty loud!

Thanks for playing, and have fun!
